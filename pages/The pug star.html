The pug who wanted to be a star!

When her best friend Chloe gets a starring role in the school show, Peggy the pug couldn't be prouder. But soon, Chloe is too busy with rehearsals to play with Peggy.Missing her friend, Peggy tries out for a part in the show - and unexpectedly becomes famous!When Peggy the pug's friend Chloe is cast as Wendy in the school production of PETER PAN, Peggy feels left out. Chloe is too busy learning lines and going to rehearsals to play with her. So Peggy sets out to show Chloe that she has star quality, too. When a hilarious video of Peggy goes viral, Peggy becomes a celebrity overnight. Suddenly, she can't even go for a a walk to the park without being stopped for a selfie. Maybe fame isn't all it's cracked up to be . . . But when Chloe gets stage fright, Peggy knows the show must go on! Question: Is being a star everything Peggy dreamed it would be?
